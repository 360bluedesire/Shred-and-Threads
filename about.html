About Us

Welcome to Shred and Threads

We are a passionate team based in the Bay Area, dedicated to crafting high-quality clothing and skateboards that reflect the vibrant culture of our community.

Our clothing line embodies the spirit of urban style and comfort, blending modern trends with local influences to create apparel that resonates with our customers.

Similarly, our skateboard designs are meticulously crafted for performance and style, catering to both beginners and seasoned skaters alike.

At our core, we value creativity, sustainability, and inclusivity. Through our products, we aim to inspire individuals to express themselves freely and confidently.
